Privacy Policy for POV Guitar

Effective Date: October 31, 2025

POV Guitar (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information when you use the POV Guitar mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide, personalize, and improve your experience using POV Guitar.

a. App Usage and Interaction Data

  • Anonymous analytics on feature usage, scale selections, and chord visualization frequency (collected via Apple and Firebase Analytics).
  • Performance and diagnostic information to help improve app reliability and UI performance.

b. Subscription and Purchase Data

  • Apple ID-linked purchase information such as product ID, transaction ID, and subscription status, collected via Apple’s in-app purchase system.
  • We do not receive or store credit card or payment credentials.

c. User Communications and Feedback

  • Information you voluntarily provide when contacting support or submitting feature requests, such as email address and message content.

POV Guitar does not collect sensitive categories of information such as location data, biometrics, or microphone recordings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the core POV Guitar experience (scale visualization, chord generation, AI customization, etc.).
  • Ensure subscription verification and feature access.
  • Analyze app performance and enhance usability.
  • Respond to user inquiries and feedback.
  • Comply with App Store policies and applicable laws.

We never sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using reputable third-party infrastructure (such as Apple Cloud and Firebase). We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

Data is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described above or as required by law.

4. Data Sharing

We may share limited data with:

  • Apple, for processing in-app purchases and verifying subscriptions.
  • Service Providers that assist with analytics and app improvement, under strict confidentiality obligations.

We do not share personal data with advertisers, social networks, or marketing agencies.

5. Children’s Privacy

POV Guitar is intended for users aged 16 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If such information is discovered, it will be promptly deleted.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to or deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent to data processing.
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

Requests can be submitted by emailing venanziz@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted within the app or on our official website. Continued use of POV Guitar after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
